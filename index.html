Welcome to the Peradeniya – Illinois – Sivananthan Collaboration Center

The Peradeniya–Illinois–Sivananthan Collaboration Center (PISCC) is a proposed institutional mechanism designed to formalize, coordinate, and expand a long-standing and highly productive partnership between the University of Peradeniya (Sri Lanka), the University of Illinois System (USA), and Sivananthan Laboratories Inc. (USA).

This initiative brings together years of academic cooperation, scientific exchange, and capacity building into a structured framework that supports long-term sustainability, strategic growth, and global impact.

The collaboration itself is not new — it is the continuation and consolidation of relationships that date back more than fifty years. What began in the 1970s as agricultural development cooperation and early medical education support has gradually evolved into a wide-ranging, multidisciplinary partnership covering agricultural innovation, medical education reform, veterinary science, renewable energy research, and advanced technology development.

The Center is intended to ensure that these activities are no longer dependent on informal coordination, but instead operate through a coherent institutional structure that enhances efficiency, accountability, and scalability.
