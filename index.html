Welcome to the Peradeniya – Illinois – Sivananthan Collaboration Center

The Peradeniya–Illinois–Sivananthan Collaboration Center (PISCC) is a proposed institutional mechanism designed to formalize, coordinate, and expand a long-standing and highly productive partnership between the University of Peradeniya (Sri Lanka), the University of Illinois System (USA), and Sivananthan Laboratories Inc. (USA).

This initiative brings together years of academic cooperation, scientific exchange, and capacity building into a structured framework that supports long-term sustainability, strategic growth, and global impact. The collaboration dates back more than fifty years, beginning in the 1970s with agricultural development cooperation and early medical education support.

Over time, this relationship has expanded to include agricultural innovation, medical education reform, veterinary science, renewable energy research, and advanced technology development. The Center ensures these activities operate through a coherent institutional structure that enhances efficiency, accountability, and scalability.
