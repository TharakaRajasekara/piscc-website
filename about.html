About Us

The vision, mission, and institutional framework of the Peradeniya–Illinois–Sivananthan Collaboration Center.

Overview

The Peradeniya–Illinois–Sivananthan Collaboration Center (PISCC) is a proposed institutional mechanism designed to formalize, coordinate, and expand a long-standing and highly productive partnership between the University of Peradeniya (Sri Lanka), the University of Illinois System (USA), and Sivananthan Laboratories Inc. (USA).

This initiative brings together years of academic cooperation, scientific exchange, and capacity building into a structured framework that supports long-term sustainability, strategic growth, and global impact.

The collaboration itself is not new; rather, it is the continuation and consolidation of relationships that date back more than fifty years. What began in the 1970s as agricultural development cooperation and early medical education support has gradually evolved into a wide-ranging, multidisciplinary partnership covering agricultural innovation, medical education reform, veterinary science, renewable energy research, and advanced technology development.

Why a Formal Center?

Despite the success of these initiatives, coordination has largely depended on informal arrangements and individual leadership. While this has enabled flexibility and rapid engagement, it is not sufficient for long-term sustainability or systematic expansion.

As the scope of collaboration continues to grow across multiple disciplines and institutions, there is a clear need for a formal institutional mechanism. The proposed Center addresses this need by providing a structured platform for coordination, governance, and strategic planning.

It will bring together all stakeholders under a unified framework, ensuring that projects are properly managed, resources are efficiently utilized, and outcomes are systematically monitored. It will also strengthen transparency, accountability, and institutional continuity — allowing the collaboration to move beyond ad-hoc engagement toward a more sustainable and scalable model.

Center Goals

  • Establish a formal governance structure for all collaborative activities
  • Ensure long-term sustainability of the partnership beyond individual leadership
  • Enable strategic growth and scalability across new disciplines
  • Strengthen transparency, accountability, and institutional continuity
  • Position PISCC as a globally relevant model for university-industry collaboration
  • Generate meaningful and lasting impact in education, science, and society

Vision Forward

The Peradeniya–Illinois–Sivananthan Collaboration Center represents the evolution of a long-standing partnership into a modern, structured, and globally relevant institution. It reflects a shared commitment to advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, and addressing complex global challenges through interdisciplinary collaboration.

By consolidating decades of experience and expanding into new areas of research and development, the Center aims to position itself as a leading example of how universities and industry partners can work together across borders to generate meaningful and lasting impact in education, science, and society.
